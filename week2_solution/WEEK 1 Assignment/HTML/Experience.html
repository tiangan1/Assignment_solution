Spring in New York City brings warmer temperatures, the start of baseball season in the Bronx and Queens and a palpable sense of optimism on the streets and sidewalks as residents shed their winter coats. It's your chance to admire cherry blossoms at botanic gardens, take nature-filled walks in Central Park and Prospect Park and-like a true New Yorker-enjoy a long, lazy brunch at an outdoor table.
